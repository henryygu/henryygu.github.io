# Oban!

Located right by the sea, you'd think that Oban would have excellent sea food restaurants right? Well I have no idea since I've always gone to this stall by the ferry station. Food is decently cheap but it tastes so incredibly awesome. They have a selection of platters where you got a bit of everything. 

What did steal the show was the garlic butter mussels. That garlic sauce was incredible. You just have to go.
